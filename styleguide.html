Making the perfect boiled egg

You'll need

For the eggs
2 eggs, at room temperature
Buttered toast fingers, to serve
For the seasoned salt
2 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp cracked black pepper
1/4 tsp ground cumin

Method

  1. Place the eggs in a small saucepan and cover with cold water. Cover and bring the water to the boil over high heat. Reduce heat immediately to medium and gently boil, uncovered, stirring occasionally, for 1 minute per egg for a soft-boiled egg, 2 minutes per egg for a medium-boiled egg, and 3 minutes per egg for a hard-boiled egg.
  2. Meanwhile, to make the seasoned salt, combine the sea salt, pepper, paprika and cumin in a small dish.
  3. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the boiled eggs from the water and place in egg cups. Serve the eggs immediately sprinkled with the seasoned salt and served with the toast fingers.

Recipe by Kirsty Cassidy & Rodney Dunn for Taste.com.au
